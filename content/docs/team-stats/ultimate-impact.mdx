---
title: "Ultimate & Ability Impact"
---

The Ultimate Impact and Ability Impact cards answer a single question: **does this
hero's ultimate (or ability) actually change fight outcomes?** Both cards compare your
team's fight win rate when a hero's ult or ability was used against the win rate when
it wasn't, so you can separate real impact from the feeling that an ultimate was doing
work.

<Callout type="info">
    Both cards are available to Premium subscribers and live on the main Team Stats
    dashboard.
</Callout>

## Ultimate Impact Analysis

The Ultimate Impact Analysis card measures how much a hero's ultimate swings fights.

### How It Works

Pick a hero from the dropdown. Fuzzy search is enabled, so you can type a partial name
to jump to the hero you're looking for. The card then splits into two scenarios:

- **Ult used**: the win rate of fights where this hero's ultimate was used. For
  example, `We win 62.3% of fights`, with the fight count shown alongside
  (`2 wins – 1 loss`). A green left border indicates the scenario is favorable; a red
  left border indicates it isn't.
- **Ult not used**: the win rate of fights where the ultimate was not used, shown with
  the same color treatment and fight count.

Compare the two side-by-side. A large gap between them means the ultimate is meaningfully
tipping fights in your favor. A small gap, or a negative one, is a signal that the
ultimate isn't carrying its weight.

## Ability Impact Analysis

The Ability Impact Analysis card applies the same approach to non-ultimate abilities:
dives, shields, movement abilities, cleanses, and other cooldowns that shape fights.

Pick a hero, and the card shows the same **used** vs **not used** comparison, win rate,
fight count, and a green or red border depending on whether the scenario is favorable.

This is useful for the cooldowns that live or die on timing, a well-timed cleanse or
shield can flip a fight, and this card tells you whether yours are doing that in
practice.

## Sample-Size Guard

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
    When fewer than three fights fall into a scenario, both cards surface a warning.
    Small samples produce noisy conclusions, don't rewrite your strategy off a single
    fight.
</Callout>

Give a hero a few scrims of data before leaning on these cards for decisions.

## How Coaches Use It

- **Separate impact from vibes**: if a hero's `ult used` win rate is close to their
  `ult not used` rate, the ultimate isn't the factor people think it is. Look elsewhere
  for the reason fights are or aren't working.
- **Diagnose timing and target selection**: a flat or negative delta on a high-impact
  ultimate usually points at when it's being used and who it's being used on, not the
  ability itself.
- **Validate comp decisions**: confirm that the ultimates you drafted around are
  actually winning the fights you're drafting them for.
- **Spotlight overperformers**: abilities with a big positive delta are working. Build
  plays around them and invest practice time in the players running them.
