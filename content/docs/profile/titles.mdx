### Title Categories

**Platform Recognition**
- **Parsertime Developer**: Contributors to the platform development
- **lux.dev Employee**: Official team members
- **Beta Tester**: Early access participants
- **Here Since the Beginning**: Players who joined on launch day

**Supporter Status**
- **Supporter**: Active basic tier supporters
- **Premium Supporter**: Active premium tier supporters  
- **VIP**: Special recognition status

**Performance-Based**
- **Achieved Greatness**: Achieved #1 on any hero leaderboard
- **Serial Killer**: Ranked in top 3 for total eliminations
- **Damage Dealer**: Ranked in top 3 for total damage
- **Ultimate Medic**: Ranked in top 3 for total healing output
- **Damage Sponge**: Ranked in top 3 for damage mitigation
- **Feeder**: Ranked in top 3 for most deaths (dubious honor)
- **Chronically Online**: Ranked in top 3 for total playtime

**Special Achievements**
- **Ajax King**: Most Ajaxes (defined as a Lúcio player dying during the cast of their ultimate ability)
- **Fleta's Disciple**: Highest percentage of team's total damage output
