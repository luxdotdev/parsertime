Linked accounts allow you to use your Parsertime account and data in other applications.
Today, this powers the Parsertime Discord bot: link your Discord account to tie your slash
command activity back to your Parsertime profile.

## Discord

Linking your Discord account lets personal slash commands such as `/teams` identify you and
return data scoped to the teams you belong to on Parsertime. Public commands like `/profile`,
`/leaderboard`, `/compare`, and `/team` work without linking.

To link your Discord account, click **Link Discord** on this page and complete the OAuth flow.
Once linked, you can unlink at any time from the same page.

<Callout type="info">
    Linking your Discord account does not add the bot to any Discord server on its own. To add
    the bot to your team's server, see [Discord Bot](/discord-bot).
</Callout>

### Setting Up the Bot for a Team

If you are a team owner or manager, you can configure how the bot interacts with your Discord
server from **Team Settings → Integrations**. From there you can:

- **Select a Discord server and channel** for the bot to post notifications in.
- **Choose a role to mention** when availability reminders are posted.
- **Set the availability reminder schedule**: the day of week and time the bot posts the
  weekly reminder.
- **Toggle scrim notifications** so the bot announces new scrims in the configured channel.

For the full list of slash commands, notification types, and troubleshooting tips, see the
[Discord Bot](/discord-bot) page.
