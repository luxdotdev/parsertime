The billing page allows you to manage your billing information and subscription.
Here, you can view your subscription details, update your payment method, and more.

## Plan Overview

The plan overview section shows your current subscription plan and your usage details.

![Billing page](/billing.png)

You can see how many teams you have created, how many members you have in your teams, and how many scrims you have uploaded.

You are also able to click the "View Other Plans" button to navigate to your billing portal via Stripe.
This is where you can manage your subscription, update your payment method, and more.

If you would like to see the benefits of each plan, please see the [Pricing](https://parsertime.app/pricing) page.

## AI Credits

[AI Chat](/docs/ai-chat) is metered separately, against a prepaid **credit
balance** on your account. Your balance is shown at the top of the billing
page and in a small indicator above the chat input.

### Topping up

Top-up presets are **$5, $10, $25, and $50**. Custom amounts are accepted
above the $5 minimum. Top-ups settle through Stripe using the payment
method on file for your account; if you do not have a payment method,
adding a top-up will collect one.

### Auto-refill

Toggle **auto-refill** to keep your balance from running out mid-session.
You set two values:

- **Threshold**: when the balance drops below this, auto-refill triggers.
  Default $2.00.
- **Amount**: how much to add. Default $10.00.

Auto-refill requires a payment method on file. The system is idempotent —
two near-simultaneous chat messages that both push the balance below the
threshold will only trigger one charge.

### Transaction log

The billing page shows the last 30 days of credit activity, with one row
per event. Types include:

- **TOPUP** — manual top-up
- **AUTO_REFILL** — automated top-up
- **CHARGE** — usage by AI Chat
- **REFUND** — issued by support
- **ADJUSTMENT** — admin correction

Click through to a charge row to see the underlying chat turn it covered,
which is useful when reconciling spend.

### Low-balance warnings

A Discord notification is sent when your balance drops below **$1.00** for
the first time. A second warning is not sent until the balance is replenished
above the threshold and crosses it again.
