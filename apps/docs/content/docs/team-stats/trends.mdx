---
title: "Trends"
---

The Trends tab tracks your team's performance over time, showing win rate trends, recent form, and win/loss streaks. This tab helps identify patterns, momentum shifts, and long-term performance trajectories.

## Features

### Winrate Over Time Chart

![Winrate Over Time Chart](/winrate-over-time.png)

A line chart displaying win rate trends with customizable timeframes:

#### Features:
- **Dual Timeframe Views**: Toggle between weekly and monthly views
- **Interactive Chart**: Hover over data points for detailed information
- **Trend Analysis**: Shows average win rate and trend direction (↑ improving, ↓ declining, → stable)
- **50% Reference Line**: Visual reference line at 50% win rate
- **Data Points**: Each point shows:
  - Period (week or month)
  - Win rate percentage
  - Wins and losses for that period

#### Insights:
- Identify performance trends over time
- Spot periods of improvement or decline
- Compare weekly vs monthly patterns
- Understand long-term trajectory

### Recent Form Card

![Recent Form Card](/recent-form.png)

Detailed breakdown of recent match results:

#### Features:
- **Multiple Views**: Toggle between last 5, 10, or 20 matches
- **Match List**: Chronological list showing:
  - Match result (Win/Loss) with color coding
  - Scrim name
  - Map played
  - Date
- **Win Rate Summary**: Overall win rate for selected timeframe
- **Performance Indicator**: Visual assessment:
  - Strong recent performance (≥60% win rate)
  - Struggling recently (≤40% win rate)
  - Average performance (40-60% win rate)
- **Visual Timeline**: Color-coded dots showing win/loss pattern at a glance

#### Use Cases:
- Quickly assess current form
- Identify recent momentum
- Track consistency
- Prepare for upcoming matches based on recent performance

### Win/Loss Streaks Card

![Win/Loss Streaks Card](/win-loss-streak.png)

Analysis of consecutive wins and losses:

#### Features:
- **Current Streak**: Shows active win or loss streak
- **Longest Streaks**: Records for:
  - Longest winning streak
  - Longest losing streak
- **Streak History**: Timeline of streak patterns
- **Streak Impact**: Analysis of how streaks affect overall performance

#### Insights:
- Understand momentum patterns
- Identify when team is "hot" or "cold"
- Track consistency and volatility
- Recognize when to make strategic adjustments

## Key Insights

The Trends tab helps answer questions like:
- Is our team improving or declining over time?
- What's our current form heading into matches?
- Are we on a hot streak or cold streak?
- How consistent is our performance?
- What patterns can we identify in our win/loss history?

## Use Cases

- **Pre-Match Preparation**: Review recent form before important matches
- **Performance Review**: Analyze long-term trends during team meetings
- **Momentum Tracking**: Identify when team is performing well or needs adjustments
- **Goal Setting**: Use trend data to set realistic improvement targets
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify factors that correlate with winning or losing streaks

## Data Requirements

- Requires historical scrim data
- More accurate with larger sample sizes
- Weekly view requires at least 1 week of data
- Monthly view requires at least 1 month of data
