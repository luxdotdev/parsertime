Parsertime turns raw Overwatch 2 Workshop logs into per-player stats, hero
skill ratings (CSR), trend lines, and team breakdowns across eight dimensions.
Upload a scrim, get a dashboard within minutes. Built for the gap between when
the scrim ends and when VOD review starts.

These docs cover the surfaces you will live in as a coach, analyst, or player:
team and scrim setup, the maps and team-stats pages, the CSR and
[TSR](/docs/tsr/overview) skill rating systems, the
[Matchmaker](/docs/matchmaker) for finding scrim partners at your bracket,
the Coaching Canvas, the Availability Calendar, and the Discord bot.

## Collecting scrim logs

In Overwatch 2, enable _Gameplay > Enable Workshop Inspector Log File_:

![A screenshot of Overwatch 2 Settings](/gameplay-settings.png)

<Callout type="info">
  The Workshop Inspector only exports logs for the lobby host. Host the lobby
  so you collect logs for your team.
</Callout>

Once enabled, use Parsertime's updated version of the [ScrimTime workshop code](https://workshop.codes/DKEEH)
(`Z0ASA`) to capture the logs. Turn on `Enable Log Generator` in Workshop
Settings:

![A screenshot of the Workshop Settings](/scrim-code-settings.png)

<Callout type="warning">
  Do not change the other `Log Generator` settings. Parsertime expects the
  defaults; changes here can produce files too large to upload.
</Callout>

Logs land in your `Documents/Overwatch/Workshop` folder. Delete any log that
is only 1kb before uploading; those are incomplete and will not parse.

### What stats are collected

By default the log generator captures:

- Match start and end
- Round start and end
- Objective captures
- Control point progress increments
- Payload progress increments
- Kills and deaths
- Offensive and defensive assists
- Hero spawn
- Hero swaps
- Ultimates charged and used
- D.Va demechs and remechs
- D.Va remech charged
- Echo duplicates
- Mercy resurrects
- Player stat summary

Parsertime visualizes all of these per map.

## Uploading scrim logs

Once signed in, open the dashboard and click `Create Scrim`. Add a title,
assign a team, pick a date, and upload the logs for the first map (you can
add the rest later).

#### Invalid Log Format (500)

Search the log file for stats appearing as `****`. The log generator
occasionally fails to write a number; replace `****` with the value you
believe is correct and re-upload.

#### Internal Server Error (500)

Likely on our end. Reach out on
[Discord](https://discord.gg/svz3qhVDXM) or
[help@parsertime.app](mailto:help@parsertime.app) and we will sort it out.

#### Unauthorized (401)

Your session expired. Log out and back in. If it persists, ping us.

#### Forbidden (403)

You are not a manager of the team you are uploading the map to. If you
are a manager, log out and back in. Otherwise contact us at
[help@parsertime.app](mailto:help@parsertime.app) or on
[Discord](https://discord.gg/svz3qhVDXM).
