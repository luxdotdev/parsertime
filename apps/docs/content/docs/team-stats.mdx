The team stats page shows various stats and charts that provide insights into the team's performance.
These stats can help you understand how the team is performing, which heroes are performing well,
and which heroes are underperforming. By analyzing the stats, you can make informed decisions about
your team's strategy and focus your efforts on the heroes that are underperforming.

## Minimum Scrim Requirement

The team stats dashboard requires at least **two scrims** worth of data before it renders.
If your team has fewer than two scrims uploaded, the page shows a placeholder directing you
to upload another scrim instead of the full dashboard.

<Callout type="info">
    This applies to all users, it's a free-tier behavior, not a plan limit.
</Callout>

The reason is accuracy. With only a single scrim, Parsertime can't reliably determine which
side of a match your team played on. It needs a second scrim to cross-reference against, and
without that, side-dependent stats would be unreliable. Uploading one more scrim unlocks the
full team stats view.
