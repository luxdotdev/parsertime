Player profiles provide a comprehensive overview of a player's performance, achievements, and play style across all heroes and scrims.

![Profile Page](/profile-page.png)

## Profile Header

![Profile Header](/profile-header.png)

The profile header displays key information about the player:

### Visual Elements
- **Custom Banner**: Personalized background image (if uploaded)
- **Avatar**: Player profile picture with fallback to initials
- **Player Name**: Displayed prominently with italic, uppercase styling
- **Title**: Current equipped title shown below the name (e.g., "Developer", "Beta Tester", "Champion")
- **Supporter Badge**: Heart icon indicating supporter status:
  - **Pink Heart**: Basic Plan Supporter
  - **Golden Heart with Glow**: Premium Plan Supporter
  - No heart for free accounts

### Employee Verification
Players with `@lux.dev` email addresses display a verified "Employee Account" badge, indicating they are official team members.

## Understanding Your Profile

### Placement Requirements

To achieve placement on heroes and appear on leaderboards:
- **10 maps minimum** on a specific hero
- **60+ seconds** of playtime per map
- Brief hero swaps don't count toward placement

### SR Calculation

Your skill rating is calculated based on:
- Individual performance statistics
- Comparative ranking against other players on that hero
- Consistency across multiple maps
- Win rate and team performance factors

### Percentile Rankings

Percentile indicates where you rank among all players:
- **99th percentile**: Top 1% (Elite)
- **95th percentile**: Top 5% (Exceptional)
- **90th percentile**: Top 10% (Excellent)
- **75th percentile**: Top 25% (Very Good)
- **50th percentile**: Median (Average)

### Play Style Interpretation

Your [play style radar](/profile/play-style-indicator) helps identify strengths and areas for improvement:
- **Balanced pentagon**: Well-rounded player
- **Spiky shape**: Specialist with clear strengths and weaknesses
- **Small overall area**: Room for growth across all areas
- **Large overall area**: Strong performance across multiple dimensions

### Activity Tracking

Consistent activity (visible in the [recent activity calendar](/profile/recent-activity-calendar)) demonstrates:
- Commitment to improvement
- Reliable team member status
- Active participation in the community
- Trend analysis for performance correlation

## Tips for Profile Optimization

### Improving Your SR
- Focus on heroes where you're already strong
- Play consistently to build your placement (10+ maps)
- Review your stat fluctuations to identify improvement areas
- Compare your play style to [top-ranked players](/csr/leaderboard)

### Earning Titles
- Performance titles require consistent excellence
- Supporter titles are available through subscription
- Special titles may be earned through events or achievements
- Check the [titles & achievements](/profile/titles) section regularly for new opportunities

### Tracking Progress
- Use the activity calendar to maintain consistent play
- Monitor [stat fluctuation cards](/profile/stat-fluctuation-cards) for trend analysis
- Compare [personal records](/profile/personal-records) to identify which heroes suit your playstyle
- Pay attention to percentile changes over time
